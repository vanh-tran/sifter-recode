Onboarding Flow

What an AP team member does when they first sign up — MVP setup in ~10 minutes

1

Create account & name your company

Email + password or Google SSO. Company name + industry (pre-filled: "Freight/Logistics"). Single-tenant workspace created.

Required Company name   Optional Logo
2

Connect your invoice mailbox

OAuth connect Gmail or Outlook. The user picks which mailbox (or folder/label) receives freight invoices from carriers. The app immediately does a test fetch and shows how many emails it found.

Required At least one mailbox   Optional Add a second mailbox
After connect: "Found 47 emails in the last 30 days that may contain invoices. We'll start processing them."
3

Add your carriers & upload rate sheets

Add each carrier you work with (name + billing contact email). For each carrier, upload the contracted rate sheet (PDF or CSV). This is the baseline the audit engine checks against.

Required At least 1 carrier   Optional Rate sheet (audit works partially without it)
Carrier billing contact email is used later for sending disputes directly from the app.
4

Upload Bills of Lading (optional but recommended)

Drag-and-drop BOLs for recent shipments. The app matches BOLs to invoices automatically using BOL/PRO numbers. Without BOLs, the audit runs with rate-sheet + math checks only.

Optional Can be uploaded later, per-shipment, at any time
✓

You're live — pipeline starts immediately

The app begins processing the mailbox backlog. A progress indicator shows ingestion running in the background. First audit results appear within minutes.

Auto Historical emails processed (last 90 days by default)   Auto New emails polled every 15 min
Design principle: Steps 1–3 are the critical path. Step 4 is encouraged but never blocks. The user sees value (first findings) within ~5 minutes of completing step 3.
